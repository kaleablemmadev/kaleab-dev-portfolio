# Project folder structure

This document describes the top-level layout and purpose of each folder in this workspace.

---

## Root

- `README.md` — The "North Star" — your 10-year vision
- `ROADMAP.md` — 12-month sprint breakdown (monthly goals)
- `DAILY_LOG.md` — Your single source of truth (standup log)

---

## 01_WRITING_LAB (Writing system)

- `drafts/` — V1: cold instinct drafts
- `deconstructions/` — Pro articles analyzed
- `rewrites/` — V2/V3 improved versions
- `swipe-file/` — Analogies, hooks, structures
- `published/` — Final polished pieces sent to clients/blogs

---

## 02_CODING_PORTFOLIO (GitHub projects)

- `weather-dashboard/`
- `todo-app/`
- `curiostack-mvp/` — The React/Flutter app codebase

---

## 03_SAAS_CURIOSTACK (Product & PM)

- `product-roadmap.md` — Features, priorities, technical specs
- `user-feedback/` — Raw feedback from early users
- `pricing-models.md` — Free vs Premium vs Institutional
- `marketing-copy/` — Landing page drafts, email sequences

---

## 04_CONTENT_MEDIA (Content factory)

- `scripts/` — 15-second reel scripts (astronomy/wildlife)
- `topics-ideas.md` — Running list of 100+ content ideas
- `posting-schedule.md` — e.g. Mon: Dev, Wed: Astro, Fri: Wildlife
- `analytics/` — Views, subscribers, engagement tracking

---

## 05_FREELANCE_BUSINESS (Clients & ops)

- `clients/` — Folder per client (NDAs, briefs)
- `proposals/` — Saved proposal templates
- `invoices/` — Generated invoices (PDFs)
- `rates-history.md` — Track your rate increases over time

---

## 06_TRACKING_METRICS (Mission control)

- `MASTER_DASHBOARD.csv` — Combined revenue, subs, traffic, words
- `weekly-retrospectives/` — Week-X.md (What went well, what didn't)
- `financials.csv` — Income vs expenses (for tax/future)

---
